The tutorials are a learning rail: each one builds on the last, and you can follow
them in order from an empty workspace to writing rib code. They are warmer and more
hands-on than the [guides](../guides/), which are recipes you reach for once you
know the shape.

  <LinkCard title="Many minds, one plan" href="many-minds-one-plan/" description="Convene a room to red-team the plan a solo workflow wrote for a real app, and ship a hardened contract you can diff." />
  <LinkCard title="One manager, many tasks" href="one-manager-many-tasks/" description="Convene a magentic room: a manager decomposes a build into tasks, delegates each to a worker, and leaves a plan with no gaps and no overlap." />
